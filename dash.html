ABOUT US


 Storytelling is an essential part of human culture and has been used for centuries to convey ideas,  emotions, and experiences. PotBoiler provides a platform for individuals to share their stories with a  wider audience as well as readers to enjoy a bundle of stories, ensuring that narratives and experiences  are preserved and passed down through generations.


 It will help build communities of like-minded people, entertain the audience by narrating a wide range  of stories and provides opportunities to aspiring writers and artists to help put up their work on a larger  platform.


 PotBoiler that comprises of stories from our great Indian Epics such as The Mahabharata and The  Ramayana. These stories will provide a pathway to preserve and share these epics with the world. This  will help in maintaining a connection with the roots and historical identity of the Indian Culture.
